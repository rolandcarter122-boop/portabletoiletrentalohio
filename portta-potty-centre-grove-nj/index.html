Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical rental cost for a porta potty?

The porta potty rental cost varies based on the type of unit, rental duration, and servicing frequency. We offer competitive and transparent pricing. Please contact us for a personalized quote tailored to your specific needs.

Do you offer long-term rental options for construction projects?

Yes, we specialize in long-term porta potty rentals for construction sites, offering flexible contracts and consistent servicing schedules to ensure continuous hygiene and comfort for your crews.

How often are the porta potty serviced for corporate events?

For corporate events, servicing frequency is customized based on event duration, attendance, and unit type. We can arrange for multiple services during longer events to maintain pristine conditions for our VIP porta potty units.

Can I rent a porta potty with a handwashing sink inside?

Yes, our deluxe porta potty come with an integrated handwashing sink. We also offer separate porta potty handwashing stations for comprehensive hygiene solutions

Can I have a porta potty placed inside a building, like a warehouse or garage?

No. For two important reasons: our service trucks require direct access to the unit to pump out the tank, and the units require open, outdoor ventilation to function properly and safely.

Centre Grove, NJ Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Centre Grove is a census-designated place (CDP) located in Cumberland County, in the U.S. state of New Jersey. It is in the center part of the county, on the east side of Lawrence Township. It is bordered to the east by the city of Millville. Bridgeton, the Cumberland county seat, is 9 miles (14 km) to the northwest.
